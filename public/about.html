About Me

Hi I’m Benson. I’m a 8 year old Bichon Poodle. My story began years ago. I’m don’t remember my birth parents nor any of my littermates. I was originally found on the streets of Downey by animal control. They cleaned and fixed me up before giving me over to the good people at Wags & Walks in Santa Monica. I was then adopted by a family in the South Bay but they were mean. They hit me with sticks and made me so scared. After a month I found myself back at Wags & Walks, this time anxious and scared.

Then one day 2 humans came to visit me. They took me out of the kennel, played ball with me and took me back to their apartment. I was scared they were going to hit me again but the smaller one with long hair slept on the ground next to me the first might and made me feel better. They take me on so many walks and feed me many treats like broccoli and apples. I am very happy they adopted me.
